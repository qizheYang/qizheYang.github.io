  • IB Geography Extended Essay

    Carrying out field trips by myself, I examined the water quality, specifically transparency, concentration of dissolved salts including NO3-, NO2-, and NH3-N, EC, and TDS, and air quality, specifically concentration of CO2, CO, and amount of PM2.5, around two major water bodies: Jinji Lake, where the famous CBD surrounds, and Baitang, where an eco park is built.

    Conducted online research on government resources and agencies to gather past records. I compared these records with my measurements to conclude the overall improvements in both air and water quality in Suzhou Industrial Park over four decades.

    Received an A rank in assessment.
